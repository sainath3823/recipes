Noodles

Description

Noodles tossed with vegetables in a flavorful sauce.

Ingredient

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add the sauce to the pan and saute it.
  4. Add the vegetables to the sauce and saute it.
  5. Add the noodles, stir quickly and turn off the heat.
