Chickpea Salad

Description

Chickpeas mixed with fresh vegetables and herbs.

Ingredient

  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped coriander
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil the chickpeas and keep aside.
  2. Add the finely chopped vegetables to a bowl.
  3. Mix the vegetables and chickpeas together.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add chopped coriander and lemon juice and mix thoroughly.
