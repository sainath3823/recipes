Tofu Scramble

Description

Crumbled tofu tossed in a pan.

Ingredient

  • Tofu
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Green chilli
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Crumble the tofu into small pieces roughly.
  2. Finely chop onions and green chilli.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Saute the onions first and then add green chilli.
  5. Add some water and add the tofu and stir till cooked.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper.
